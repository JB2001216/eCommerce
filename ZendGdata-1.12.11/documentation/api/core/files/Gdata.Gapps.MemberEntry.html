\Zend_Gdata_Gapps_MemberEntry
jump to top

Package: Zend_Gdata\Gapps
Data model class for a Google Apps Member Entry.
Each member entry describes a single member within a Google Apps hosted domain. To transfer member entries to and from the Google Apps servers, including creating new entries, refer to the Google Apps service class, Zend_Gdata_Gapps. This class represents in the Google Data protocol.
